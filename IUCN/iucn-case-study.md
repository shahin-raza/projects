
# IUCN (International Union for Conservation of Nature)


## About IUCN
IUCN is the International Union for Conservation of Nature, a global environmental network that works to protect the natural world through a membership of government and civil society organizations. It is known for being the authority on the status of the natural world and the measures needed to safeguard it, producing tools like the IUCN Red List of Threatened Species. The organization works on issues like climate change, biodiversity loss, and pollution, and provides knowledge and tools to help human progress and nature conservation go hand in hand.

1. Implemented **page style variations** using **ECK components**.

4. Handled **multilingual content structure** and translation workflows.
5. Migrated the entire platform from **Drupal 7 to Drupal 9**, including:
