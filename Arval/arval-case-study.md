
# ARVAL BNPP BANK

BNP Paribas is a French multinational universal bank and financial services holding company headquartered in Paris, France.

BNPP has aquired a car leasing bussiness called ARVAL. In which they have a account called Apollo, which take care of the business for DCX (Digital & Customer Experience)

Apollo is a CMS application developed in Drupal 10 & React. The site has been developed as multisite and multilingual for different countries.

Applications has 120+ websites running on single code base and single database. The main objects of this platform is to create the content based on the component. Offer listing page based on the current scenario.

They were there types of car leasing services:
1. LTR, Long Term Rental Service (1-2 Years)
2. MTR, Medium term rental service (6-12 Months)
3. PL, Private lease (Less than 6 Months)
